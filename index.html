7 reasons your current website is costing you new patients

If three or more of these sound familiar, your website is silently sending patients to your competitor.

1
Patients can't book online — they have to call
It's 9pm. A patient with toothache decides to find a new dentist. Your competitor has online booking. You don't. They book the other guy and never call you.
2
Your reviews aren't on the homepage
90% of patients read reviews before booking. If yours are buried — or worse, missing entirely — patients move on. They want to see proof before they trust you.
3
Patients can't tell which insurance you accept
70% of Americans have dental insurance. If your site doesn't clearly list which plans you accept, patients assume you don't take theirs and look elsewhere.
4
Your site looks dated — patients notice
When choosing a healthcare provider, patients judge competence by appearance. An outdated website makes them wonder if your equipment is outdated too. Unfair, but real.
5
Your services aren't clearly explained
"General dentistry" doesn't tell patients you do Invisalign, implants, or pediatric care. Patients searching for specific services need to find them clearly — or they'll find a practice that lists them.
6
Your phone number isn't easy to find on mobile
62% of patients search on a phone. If they have to scroll, pinch-zoom, or hunt for your number, they'll close the tab and try the next listing instead.
7
New patients can't see your team or your office
Healthcare is personal. Patients want to see who they'll meet, what your office looks like, and feel comfortable before they even walk in. A trust-first design with team photos, office photos, and welcome video reduces no-shows AND increases bookings.

Insurance + cost transparency – the page most practices skip

70% of patients have dental insurance. Most practice websites bury this info – yours puts it front and center. Bigger trust signal than any testimonial.

Accepted insurance list

Every plan you accept, listed clearly. Patients self-qualify before they call – no more wasted calls about plans you don't take.

In-house membership plans

For uninsured patients, your in-house plan gets its own page. Captures the 30%+ of patients without insurance — usually overlooked by competitors.

Financing options page

CareCredit, in-house payment plans, third-party financing — listed clearly. Removes the "can I afford it?" hesitation before they call.

"What things cost" guide

Honest cost ranges for common procedures. Patients searching for "Invisalign cost near me" find you. Builds trust and reduces price-shopping calls.

Insurance verification form

Patients can submit their insurance info before they come in. Your front desk verifies coverage in advance — fewer surprises, smoother first visits.

FAQ on insurance & billing

Common questions answered upfront — "Do you bill my insurance directly?" "What if my plan doesn't cover this?" Reduces front-desk burden by 30-40%.
